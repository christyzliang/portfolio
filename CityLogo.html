Tangier, Morocco

Description of the Project

The Objective was to select an international tourist city and create a logo representing them. Tangier, Morocco is a port city that became a tourist destination because of their vibrant streets.

Design decisions included: design process

Client: Tangier, Morocco
Service: Logo
Tools: Illustrator

SKETCHES

Through research, I narrowed down three main points that makes Tangier, Tangier. The three main ideas were that Tangier is a huge port city, they have vibrant streets, and being that they are located in Africa, Tangier is extremely hot.

LOGO VERSIONS

Illustrating the three main points, I determinded that the third as a City logo will not be usable as it looks like a illustration of door. The second logo ties two main point, ports and sun. But it is not scaleable. The first logo is promising because it represents the sun, which is a huge vibrant star.

FINAL LOGO

With some finesse, I was able to achevie vibrancy, with the way the rays flow along with the colors.
