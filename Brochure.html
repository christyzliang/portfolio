TREVOR PAGLEN

Description of the Project

Create a promotional brochure for The Smithsonian Museum, featuring Trevor Paglen. He is a geographer and photographer that creates art pieces from the cables at the bottom of the sea to football field-sized antennas in space.

Design decisions included: typography, grid systems, and hierarchy.

Client: The Smithsonian Museum
Service: Print
Tools: Photoshop, and Indesign

BROCHURE

I created a gatefold brochure to represent Trevor Paglen's work. This decision came about because of the type of photography he took, he took many space and see photos. I thought it would be beneficial that once the brochure is opened, the first thing you see if his work and a quote, leading the reader deeper in.

GRID

These are the grids that I used for the cover, body copy, and back of the brochure.
