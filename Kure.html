Kure

Description of the Project

Kure is a CBD company that offers CBD products for those who suffer from chronic pain and anxiety.

The objective was to develop a corporate identity for Kure. The identity should reflect the brand along with their customers.

Client: Kure
Service: Print, Branding, Identity, Web
Tools: Photoshop, Indesign, Illustrator, and Photography

LOGO

The third eye represents unlocking something deeper within ourselves. Kure's goal is to help others unlock a sense of comfort and balance in their daily lives.

TYPOGRAPHY

Kure's focus is providing a product that relives those in anxiety and pain. Kure used mainly Avenir as the font had a huge family, it brought sense of trust to the brand and did not take away from the pattern background.

BOTTLES AND JARS

The final look of the overall packaging, consists of a vrubrant pattern and label. The design choice for the vibrancy is to add life in every bite taken.
