Book Jackets

Description of the Project

Radio Lab is a podcast that investigates a strange world in which we live in. Three podcasts were chosen to be interpreted into book jackets. The final trilogy should represent the theme and mood of the podcasts.

Design decisions included: typography, grid systems, and design process

Client: Radio Lab
Service: Print
Tools: Photoshop, and Indesign

PODCASTS

RadioLab wanted to create a trilogy from three random podcasts BUT before creating them. I had to listen to the first podcast choosen, "Famous Tumors".

THREE RENDITION

Based off on that first podcast I created three different interpretations using three different mediums. Digital, string, and paint.

CHOSEN ONE

After listening to all three podcasts, I choose to create the trilogy with string. String became the one that links them together. In all three podcasts, they talked about how there is always a reason behind each occurance that happens. Like RadioLab, they talk about investigating a strange world and their series of events.
